ACTIONS

Curb Mass Media Corruption

The media (including social media) has an important role in the fight against corruption as it can demand accountability and transparency from the public and private sectors.

Combat Court Corruption

The most common argument made for special anti-corruption courts is the need for efficient resolution of corruption cases.

Stop Business Corruption

Make anti-corruption part of your company culture and operations. Show your employees, customers and suppliers that your company has a zero-tolerance policy on bribery & corruption

Prevent Hospital Corruption

Include the use of independent agencies to investigate and punish corruption, ensuring that information about healthcare prices is clear and accessible to the public together with increasing healthcare worker salaries.

Finish Educational Corruption

Corruption in education threatens the well-being of society because it erodes social trust and worsens inequality. It sabotages development by undermining the formation of educated, competent, and ethical individuals for future leadership and the labour force
